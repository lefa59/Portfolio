This is a National Geographic series I did for an advertising class. The main focus was to bring light to the endangered species in the world. Choosing three animals of the same animal kingdom also helped show a light of the endangerment. Using a high-resolution photograph for one side then traditionally painting the other. Sending the message “Don’t let them become just another illustration.” Sends a bigger overall message to the series. Along with making the word "illustration" in a decrotive font. Both image and font combined gave the overall project its core meaning.
