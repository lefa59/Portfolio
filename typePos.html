In this work I learned all about how important type is in the world. Before this project I wasn’t really a type person I didn’t really see the reason to have words when you can just make a drawing or use a photo to explain those words. Throughout this whole project I learned the history and the importance of type and why we use it in this day and age. Using Adobe InDesign and Illustrator I marked and showed the important and different parts of the letters. Learning how to tell the difference between fonts can help a designer figure out what font another designer used on a project.
